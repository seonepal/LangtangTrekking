About Langtang Trekking

The Langtang Valley Trek is one of Nepal’s most beautiful and accessible Himalayan adventures. Located just north of Kathmandu, this trek combines breathtaking mountain views, Tibetan-inspired culture, and diverse flora and fauna. Perfect for both beginner and seasoned trekkers, Langtang offers an authentic Himalayan experience.

Highlights of the Trek

Scenic Landscapes

Snow-capped peaks, lush forests, and the stunning Langtang Glacier.

Cultural Experience

Immerse in Tamang and Tibetan culture, monasteries, and traditions.

Adventure Awaits

A moderate trek suitable for adventure seekers with 7–10 day itineraries.

Itinerary Snapshot

  • Day 1: Drive from Kathmandu to Syabrubesi
  • Day 2–4: Trek through Lama Hotel, Langtang Village
  • Day 5: Explore Kyanjin Gompa
  • Day 6–7: Return trek to Syabrubesi
  • Day 8: Drive back to Kathmandu
